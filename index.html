How it works

Three simple steps to better decisions

1

Create a session

Name your decision and add the choices you're considering. Add emojis and notes for context.

2

Vote together

Share your screen or device. Everyone votes with +1 or -1 buttons. Watch results update in real-time.

3

See the winner

Visual bars show vote distribution. The leading choice is highlighted. Make your decision with confidence.

Your data stays yours

QuickVote uses IndexedDB to store all sessions and votes directly in your browser. No data is sent to any server. No tracking, no analytics, no third parties.

Note: Clearing your browser data will remove all sessions. This is a local tool—there's no cloud backup or account system.
